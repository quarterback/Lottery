Chip Window Simulator Bid Standardization · 22-game chip window · all 30 teams

Activates at game 60 for all 30 teams. Each team starts with 100 chips and wagers between 10–25 chips per game. Winner gains the opponent's wager; loser loses their own — chips can go negative. Each lottery team may use their double once on any home game — no chip minimum required. Draft odds split two ways: the 5 worst-record teams each hold a guaranteed 10% floor; all 14 lottery teams compete for a remaining 50% chip pool distributed by final totals. Tanking is structurally impossible.
