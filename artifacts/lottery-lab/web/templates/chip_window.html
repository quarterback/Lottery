Chip Window Simulator Bid Standardization · 22-game chip window

Activates at game 60 for all non-top-12 teams. Each team starts with 100 chips and must wager at least 10 per game (10 or 25). A win returns the wager; a loss permanently deducts it. Teams finishing with >100 chips may double — forfeit 100 chips to double the balance. Final lottery odds are proportional to chips, floored at current NBA record-based odds. Tanking is structurally impossible.
